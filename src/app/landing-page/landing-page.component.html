Curriculum Management System
Curriculum revision is one of the key factors for a program's success. Curriculum revisions undergo different stages for it to be effective. The Curriculum Management System (CMS) aspires to offer a coherent, effective, and up to date curriculum. The process includes creating a committee that will manage the said system.
